Naufal Aufaa Abyan, akrab disapa Opayy, adalah co-founder OZ Creative Agency yang menggabungkan dua keahlian yang jarang ditemukan dalam satu orang: kemampuan teknis menulis smart contract on-chain, dan kemampuan bisnis untuk membawa produk itu ke pasar.

Di sisi development, Opayy menulis kontrak Rust/Anchor untuk Solana dan Solidity untuk Ethereum — termasuk arsitektur inti di balik Axeon Management Protocol. Di sisi bisnis, ia yang menyusun strategi produk, pitch deck, dan menjalin partnership untuk OZ Creative Agency.

Meski merangkap dua peran, Opayy dan Zenn tetap setara secara skill teknis — pembagian ini murni soal fokus, bukan soal siapa yang lebih jago.

Naufal Aufaa Abyan, known as Opayy, is co-founder of OZ Creative Agency, combining two skill sets rarely found in one person: the technical ability to write on-chain smart contracts, and the business ability to bring that product to market.

On the development side, Opayy writes Rust/Anchor contracts for Solana and Solidity for Ethereum — including the core architecture behind Axeon Management Protocol. On the business side, he shapes product strategy, pitch decks, and partnerships for OZ Creative Agency.

Despite wearing two hats, Opayy and Zenn remain equal in technical skill — the split is purely about focus, not about who's more capable.
