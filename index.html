about us

Dua nama, satu standar kerja.Two names, one standard of work.

OZ bukan singkatan perusahaan — itu gabungan dua alias kami: Opayy dan Zenn. Kami berdua setara secara skill teknis; bedanya, Opayy merangkap sebagai business development di samping development, sementara Zenn full fokus sebagai core developer.

Kami tidak menjual buzzword Web3. Setiap project yang kami pegang punya smart contract yang bisa kamu baca, repository yang bisa kamu clone, dan demo yang bisa kamu coba sendiri sebelum percaya pada klaim kami.

Berbasis di Jambi, Indonesia — bekerja untuk ekosistem Solana dan Ethereum.

OZ isn't a company acronym — it's a fusion of our two aliases: Opayy and Zenn. We're equal in technical skill; the difference is Opayy also handles business development alongside development, while Zenn focuses fully as core developer.

We don't sell Web3 buzzwords. Every project we take on has a smart contract you can read, a repository you can clone, and a demo you can try yourself before trusting our claims.

Based in Jambi, Indonesia — building for the Solana and Ethereum ecosystems.
